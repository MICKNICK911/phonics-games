Phonics Decoding Tool

An interactive tool for visualizing phonics patterns and decoding strategies

How to Use:

  • Enter a word and click "Show Word" to display it
  • Select a tool from the panel on the right (or bottom on mobile)
  • Click on letters to apply annotations
  • For group curves: Click first letter, then last letter to create arc
  • Save your annotations with "Save Word"
  • Check your annotations with "Check Answer"
