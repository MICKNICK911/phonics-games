📖 How to Play

  1. Tap adjacent letters that form a single sound (e.g., "sh", "ee", "p").
  2. Click "Found Sound" to lock them – they get a unique colour.
  3. Cover every letter in the word with a sound group.
  4. Click "Check Sounds" to verify your answer.
  5. ✅ Correct → a "Well Done" popup appears for 2 seconds, then next word.
  6. ❌ Wrong → a "Try Again" popup shows for 2 seconds. You lose one ❤️.
  7. After 3 wrong attempts → game over. Finish all 10 words to win!

    8. Press Esc or tap outside to close
