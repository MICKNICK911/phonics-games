Phonics Blends

Master consonant blends to improve reading and spelling skills

How to Use This Resource

Welcome to the Phonics Blends educational tool! This resource helps you learn and practice consonant blends, which are two or three consonants that appear together in a word while keeping their individual sounds.

Understanding Blends

  • Beginning Blends (in blue) appear at the start of words.
  • Ending Blends (in red) appear at the end of words.
  • Some blends like "st", "sk", and "sp" can appear at both the beginning and end of words.

How to Practice

  • Read the words in the tables aloud, focusing on the blend sounds.
  • Use the interactive game section to test your knowledge.
  • Click on letters to highlight them, then click "Check Word" to see if you've found a blend.
  • You have 3 attempts per word to find all blends.

Tips for Success

  • Practice a little every day for best results.
  • Say the words out loud to reinforce the sounds.
  • Try to think of other words that use the same blends.
