How to Play

• The leftmost circle is 0, the rightmost is a a random number.
• The GREEN circle is your target.
• Tap the ▼ arrow below it and guess the correct number the green circle holds.
• Choose from three options.
• Correct → next level | Wrong → game over.
• You have 60 seconds per level.
