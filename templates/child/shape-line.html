How to Play

• A row of 20 → 40 shapes follows a pattern (level‑dependent).
• One shape is hidden (transparent) – tap the ☝️ finger below it.
• Choose the correct shape from three options.
• Correct → next level | Wrong → game over.
• Each level gives you seconds.
• First shape is always visible – find the logic!
• Patterns become more complex as you advance.
